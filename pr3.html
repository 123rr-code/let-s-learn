How to operate a computer?

Starting

Power On: Press the power button located on the computer case or keyboard to turn on the computer. Laptops typically have a power button on the keyboard.
Login : If required, enter your username and password to log into the operating system (e.g., Windows, macOS, Linux).
Desktop or Home Screen: After logging in, you will see the desktop or home screen. This is the main interface where you can access applications and files

Shutting Down or Restarting:

Shut Down: Click on the Start menu (Windows) or Apple menu (macOS), then select "Shut Down" or "Restart" to turn off or restart the computer
