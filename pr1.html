What is computer ?

COMPUTER:“Common Operating Machine Purposely Used for Technological and Educational Research”.

A computer is a programmable electronic device that can process data via instructions called programs.It can store, retrieve, and manipulate data, performing tasks with incredible speed and accuracy. Computers consist of hardware components such as processors, memory, and storage devices, as well as software that provides the instructions for operation. They enable a wide range of functions, from simple calculations to complex simulations and communication across global networks. Computers have revolutionized industries, education, and everyday life, becoming essential tools for work, entertainment, and information access.
